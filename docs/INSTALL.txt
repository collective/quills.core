Installation
============

This package is part of the Quills product and is automatically included
when Quills is installed. Have a look at `Products.Quills`_ or 
`Products.QuillsEnabled`_.

.. _Products.Quills: http://pypi.python.org/pypi/Products.Quills/
.. _Products.QuillsEnabled: http://pypi.python.org/pypi/Products.QuillsEnabled/
